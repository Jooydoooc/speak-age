4. Filler words

These crutch words damage your fluency score. Replace them with the natural alternative or a brief pause.

Avoid
Try instead
um, uh, er
a brief pause
like (overused)
for example, such as
you know
in other words
basically
essentially, in short
stuff / things
issues, factors, aspects
very + adjective
stronger adjective (huge, vital, crucial)
I think (repeated)
in my view, from my perspective
good / bad
beneficial / detrimental
